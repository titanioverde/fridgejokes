What is this? (I don't even)

Fridge Jokes is an entertainment web application oriented to people who have played the videogame Persona 4.

During most ingame days, the main character has the option to check the fridge in his home. He may find a little surprise, usually food of different kinds, and the player will decide to interact (eat, drink, etc.) with it. Sometimes it will result in some bonus, or will turn the character sick.

Some certains related videos on Youtube have a significant amount of comments mimicking the text shown during these interactions with the fridge, becoming jokes on their own. And the raison d'Ãªtre of this page is to collect these jokes and to show them in a fashion similar to the original videogame.

Why create a whole website just for jokes that few people will really understand?

For me, the developer, was a nice excuse to put my beginner skills in action, being this my second web project shown to the public.

Thus, I hope this web application will serve me well in my future professional portfolio.

Also, the internet is full of humour of all kinds, and there is always place for more.

How do I stop the music?

Press on the music bar on your left. A Youtube window will smoothly show up at your control. Click on the same bar to hide it again.

Which framework did you use for this app?

None, except if you consider jQuery a framework, which is being used for asynchronous calls (AJAX) and some animations. The pages are processed on PHP, and use some HTML5 and CSS3 features.

Another library used here is Prefix Free, to ease CSS3 compatibility.

"Persona 4"?

Shin Megami Tensei: Persona 4. A relatively famous japanese RPG from Atlus, for PlayStation 2 and PlayStation Vita. It also has its own animation series.

The contents in this web application are mostly based on the original PlayStation 2 edition, although it has some little references to its previous issue, Persona 3.

I haven't played any of this. Will I laugh anyway?

You can try. Many jokes, as usual on the internet, are crossed with other memes. Some even can be easy to understand for everyone.

I'm afraid of spoilers.

Some jokes, when sent to our database, are marked as "potential spoiler". You will not see Spoiler jokes by default.

To be able to see them, you must register as a new user (or login) and change its option on the Settings.

Can I undo a vote?

Of course. Click on the vote again (now in a different color) to discard it.
